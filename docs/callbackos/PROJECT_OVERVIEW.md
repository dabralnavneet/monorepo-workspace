# CallbackOS — Project Overview

## What is CallbackOS?

CallbackOS is an **end-to-end interview experience platform** for job seekers in India. It helps candidates manage their entire job search lifecycle — from uploading and tailoring resumes to tracking interview funnels and discovering job listings across top Indian job boards.

## Problem Statement

Job seekers in India face fragmented, painful workflows:

1. **Resume customization** — Manually tailoring resumes for each job description is tedious and error-prone.
2. **Application tracking** — Most candidates use messy spreadsheets or memory to track where they've applied and what stage they're in.
3. **Job discovery** — Listings are spread across Naukri, LinkedIn, Instahyre, Wellfound, Cutshort, Hirect, Internshala, and company career pages. No single platform unifies them.
4. **Interview preparation** — Candidates lack a structured way to prepare based on specific JDs.

## Solution

CallbackOS provides a unified platform with these core features:

### MVP Features (Phase 1)
- **Resume Upload & Parsing** — Upload PDF/DOCX resumes; extract structured data (skills, experience, education).
- **AI-Powered JD Tailoring** — Paste a job description, get an optimized resume variant with a match score.
- **Application Pipeline (Conversion-First)** — Track applications through stages: Saved → Applying → Applied → **Callback ✨** → Interviewing → Offer 🎉. No "Rejected" stage — cold applications are quietly archived. The hero metric is **callback rate**.
- **Job Listings Aggregation** — Curated listings from top Indian job boards presented in a unified feed.

### Future Features (Phase 2+)
- AI-powered mock interviews
- Company-specific interview prep guides
- Referral network (request/offer referrals)
- Salary benchmarking
- Browser extension for one-click apply
- Community features

## Target Audience

- **Primary**: Active job seekers in India (fresh graduates, 0-5 years experience, career switchers)
- **Secondary**: Passive job seekers who want to keep their pipeline warm
- **Tertiary**: Recruiters/companies seeking qualified candidates (B2B opportunity)

## Monetization Strategy

| Revenue Stream | Model | Details |
|----------------|-------|---------|
| Freemium Subscriptions | B2C | Free tier (basic features), Pro (₹299-499/mo), Premium (₹799-999/mo) |
| Sponsored Listings | B2B | Companies pay to be featured in the job feed |
| Recruitment Leads | B2B | Sell qualified candidate leads to companies |
| Affiliate Commissions | B2B2C | Earn from interview prep platform referrals |
| Campus Licenses | B2B | Bulk licenses for colleges and bootcamps |

## Key Metrics to Track

- **User Acquisition**: Sign-ups, DAU/MAU, referral rate
- **Engagement**: Resumes tailored per user, applications tracked, job listings viewed
- **Conversion**: Free → Pro conversion rate, interview callback improvement rate
- **Revenue**: MRR, ARPU, churn rate
